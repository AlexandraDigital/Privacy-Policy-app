🍪 Cookie-Care-Joy | Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 9, 2026

The short version: Cookie-Care-Joy doesn't collect, store, or share your personal data. Everything stays on your device. We believe your self-care journey is private — and it should stay that way. 🌸

📱 What This App Is

Cookie-Care-Joy is a self-care and productivity app that runs entirely in your web browser. It is hosted as a static website on GitHub Pages and does not use any servers to store your tasks, habits, or personal information.

🗂️ Data We Collect

We collect no personal data. Cookie-Care-Joy does not:

  • Create accounts or require sign-up
  • Track your name, email, or location
  • Use cookies for tracking or analytics
  • Send your data to any external server
  • Use third-party analytics tools (e.g. Google Analytics)

💾 Local Storage

Cookie-Care-Joy saves the following data locally on your device only, using your browser's localStorage:

  • Your tasks and self-care activities
  • Your cookie count and trophy progress
  • Your daily intentions
  • Your selected jar theme and alarm sound
  • Your Golden Bakery Pass unlock status
This data never leaves your device. It is not sent to us or any third party. You can clear it at any time by clearing your browser's site data.

💳 Payments (Golden Bakery Pass)

If you purchase the Golden Bakery Pass, payment is processed securely through PayPal. We do not receive, store, or have access to your credit card or bank details.

After payment, you will receive an unlock code by email. That email address is used only to generate your unique unlock code and is not stored, shared, or used for marketing.

PayPal has its own privacy policy which you can review at paypal.com.

🔔 Notifications & Alarms

Cookie-Care-Joy uses browser-based alarms that run locally in your browser tab. No push notifications are sent to your device, and no notification data is collected or transmitted.

🧒 Children's Privacy

Cookie-Care-Joy is suitable for general audiences and does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. Since we collect no personal data at all, there is no risk of children's data being stored or shared.

🔗 Third-Party Links

The app may contain a link to PayPal for payment processing. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of external websites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before proceeding.

✏️ Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this privacy policy, the new version will be posted on this page with an updated date. Since we collect no data, changes are unlikely to affect you in any meaningful way.
